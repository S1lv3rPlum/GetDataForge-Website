What It Does

App Overview

GearSmith was inspired by a classic car enthusiast who needed a simple way to track the work done on their vehicles. Whether you're a car enthusiast, a weekend mechanic, or just someone who likes keeping their vehicles maintained, GearSmith keeps everything organized in one place.

The app works offline-first — log maintenance even without internet access, and it syncs automatically whenever a connection is available. Perfect for households with multiple vehicles and multiple people helping maintain them.

What's Included

Features

🚗

Multiple Vehicles

Add and manage every vehicle in your household — cars, trucks, motorcycles, whatever you drive.

🔩

Maintenance Logs

Track every service, repair, and modification with dates, mileage, costs, and shop names.

📶

Offline-First Sync

Log maintenance anywhere — no connection needed. Syncs automatically when you're back online.

Multiple family members can view and log service history for shared vehicles.

🧾

Receipt Attachments

Service Reminders Coming Soon

Cost Tracking Coming Soon

Monitor maintenance spend per vehicle over time to budget smarter.
