GearSmith

A vehicle maintenance tracker that keeps your cars organized. Originally built for a classic Corvette enthusiast, GearSmith tracks maintenance, receipts, modifications, and more — with shared logs for everyone who helps care for your vehicles.

Stock Hammer

A simple inventory and parts tracker for makers and small shops. Keep counts accurate, reduce guesswork, and make restocking decisions easier.

A 3D printing filament tracker built for small businesses. Monitor spool weights, usage, and remaining stock to avoid mid-print failures and improve inventory planning.
