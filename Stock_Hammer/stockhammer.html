App Overview

Stock Hammer is designed to simplify inventory management and streamline your point of sales operations. Whether you run a small retail store or manage a warehouse, Stock Hammer helps you keep track of stock levels, sales, and purchases in one easy-to-use app. Although still in development, our goal is to empower businesses with real-time insights and efficient transaction tracking.

Features

  • Track inventory levels.
  • Manage sales and purchases seamlessly.
  • Generate reports to analyze sales trends.
  • Offline-first design to let you manage inventory without internet connection.
  • Future updates: Integrations with popular e-commerce platforms and advanced analytics dashboard.
