What It Does

App Overview

Stock Hammer is designed to simplify inventory management and streamline point of sale operations. Whether you run a small retail store, a maker shop, or manage a warehouse, Stock Hammer helps you keep track of stock levels, sales, and purchases in one easy-to-use app.

Built with an offline-first design so you can manage inventory without an internet connection — syncing automatically when you're back online. Our goal is to empower small businesses with real-time insights and efficient transaction tracking.
