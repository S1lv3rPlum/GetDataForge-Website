Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9/1/25

GearSmith ("the App") is developed to help users track maintenance on their vehicles. This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account or log in, we collect your email address through Firebase Authentication.
  • Usage Data: Firebase may collect non-identifying information such as device type, operating system, crash logs, and app performance metrics.
  • Maintenance Data: Any information you enter into the App (e.g., vehicle details, maintenance records) is stored securely in Firebase.

2. How We Use Information

  • To allow you to log in and access your account across devices.
  • To sync your data between devices and/or household members.
  • To improve app stability and performance.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share data with trusted third-party services (Firebase) that provide hosting, authentication, and database functionality.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
