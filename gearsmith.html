App Overview

GearSmith was inspired by a classic car enthusiast looking for a simple way to track the work done on their vehicles. Whether you're a car enthusiast, a weekend mechanic, or just someone who loves taking care of your ride, GearSmith helps you keep track of all your vehicles in one place. The app works offline, so you can log maintenance even without internet access, and syncs online automatically whenever a connection is available ‚Äî perfect for households with multiple cars.

Features

  • Add and manage multiple vehicles, including household cars.
  • Log maintenance tasks, service history, and work done on each vehicle.
