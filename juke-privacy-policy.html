Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

The Juke is designed to provide a fun, simple way to browse songs and submit live music requests at participating venues. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your information safe. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Song Requests: The songs you request during a show.
  • Optional Tips Information: If you choose to tip a performer, you are redirected to Venmo. The Juke does not store any payment information.
  • Device Information: Basic device details such as model, OS version, and app version (used only to improve app performance).
  • Crash Logs & Analytics: Anonymous diagnostic data used to fix bugs and improve the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal names
  • No email addresses
  • No contacts or photos
  • No location data
  • No payment information

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your song requests to the performing artist
  • To ensure the app works correctly during live events
  • To improve performance and fix technical issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Your active song request is shown to the performing artist or venue
  • Anonymous analytics may be sent to app performance tools

Data Retention

Song requests may be temporarily stored until the event ends. No long-term personal data is kept.

Your Rights

You may request that we delete any data associated with your usage of The Juke. To request deletion, contact us using the link below.
