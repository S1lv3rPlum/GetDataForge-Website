Privacy Policy - Band & Venue Manager App

Last Updated: January 2025

The Juke Band & Venue Manager App allows performers and venues to manage song lists, events, and requests from the audience. We take privacy seriously and are committed to keeping your information safe. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account/Profile Information: Band or venue name, email, and optional profile details to manage your events.
  • Set Lists & Song Data: Songs you make available for requests, including any metadata (artist, title, order).
  • Event Data: Event dates, location names, and performance details.
  • Device Information: Basic device details such as model, OS version, and app version (used only to improve app performance).
  • Crash Logs & Analytics: Anonymous diagnostic data used to fix bugs and improve the app.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your set lists and song availability to fans using the customer-facing Juke app.
  • To manage and update events or multiple venues.
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Your set list and active songs are visible to fans using the customer-facing Juke app.
  • Anonymous app analytics may be sent to performance monitoring tools.

Data Retention

Set lists and event data are stored as long as your account is active. You may update or delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all stored data at any time. To request deletion, contact us using the button below.
