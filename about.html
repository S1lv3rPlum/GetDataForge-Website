The Longer Version

How I Got Here

My journey into app development started as a practical problem-solver's detour. Years of manufacturing process work and Lean Six Sigma projects built a habit of asking: why is this hard, and how could it be easier?

VBA macros scratched that itch for a long time. But eventually the Excel sandbox felt too small. DataForge is what happened when I decided to actually learn to build things properly — Android apps, web apps, Firebase backends, and more.

Every app here started as a real need: a friend needed to track a classic Corvette's maintenance, a kid needed a safe way to look up unicorns, a band needed a way to let the crowd request songs. That's the DataForge philosophy — practical tools for real life. 🔧🔥
