Welcome! Spark Search is a safe image and video search engine designed specifically for young children (ages 3+). With built-in parental controls and pre-filtered content, you can let your little ones explore their interests safely while you maintain oversight.

Getting Started

Set up Spark Search in just a few minutes to create a safe browsing environment for your child:

  • Create a parent account: Set up your secure login to manage settings
  • Add your child's profile: Create a kid-friendly profile with their favorite interests
  • Configure safety settings: Customize filtering levels and content restrictions
  • Set up the banned word list: Add any additional words or phrases you want to block

Pro Tip: Start with strict filtering and gradually adjust as your child gets older and you become more comfortable with the app.

For Kids: How to Search

Spark Search is designed to be simple enough for even the youngest users:

Searching for Images:

  1. Type what you want to see in the search box (like "puppies" or "unicorns")
  2. Click the search button
  3. Scroll through the safe, pre-filtered images
  4. Click any image to see it bigger

Searching for Videos:

  1. Use the same search box to find videos
  2. Switch to the "Videos" tab
  3. Click to watch safe, kid-friendly videos

What Kids Can Search: Animals, toys, cartoons, colors, shapes, nature, and any other kid-friendly topics. All results are pre-filtered for safety!

Coloring Page Feature

Turn search results into fun coloring activities!

How It Works:

  • Search a word
  • Click the "Make Coloring Page" button
  • The images shown are black-and-white outlines
  • Your child can color it digitally using touch or mouse
  • Save or print the finished artwork!

Coloring Tools:

  • Choose from a rainbow of colors
  • Adjust brush size for big or small areas
  • Use the eraser to fix mistakes
  • Undo/redo buttons for easy corrections
  • Save completed coloring pages to a gallery

Parent Tip: The coloring feature is perfect for quiet time, travel, or creative play without screen time worries!

Parental Controls & Oversight

Stay informed about your child's searches and maintain control over their experience:

Search History & Reports:

  • View search history: See everything your child has searched for
  • Weekly reports: Get summaries of their most common searches and interests

Managing the Banned Word List:

  • Access the banned word list from parent settings
  • Add custom words or phrases to block
  • Review and remove words as needed
  • Import common blocked word lists for comprehensive protection

Privacy Note: Search history is stored locally on your device only. We never collect or share your child's search data.

Built-In Safety Features

Spark Search includes multiple layers of protection:

  • Pre-filtered results: All content is screened before it reaches your child
  • No ads or tracking: Completely ad-free experience with zero data collection
  • No external links: Kids can't accidentally leave the safe environment
  • Age-appropriate content only: Results tailored for preschool to early elementary ages
  • Automatic word filtering: Inappropriate terms are blocked automatically

Customizing Settings

Tailor Spark Search to your family's needs:

Session Limits:

  • Set daily time limits
  • Schedule available hours
  • Receive notifications when time is up

Tips for Parents

  • Search together at first: Spend time using Spark Search with your child to teach them how to search safely and respectfully.
  • Review search history regularly: Check weekly to understand your child's interests and ensure they're using the app appropriately.
  • Encourage creativity: Use the coloring feature to turn screen time into creative time!
  • Update banned words: As your child grows and their vocabulary expands, review and update your banned word list.
  • Use it as a learning tool: When your child searches for something, use it as a conversation starter about that topic.
  • Set clear boundaries: Establish rules about when and how long they can use Spark Search, just like any other screen time.

Troubleshooting

Search isn't showing results?

  • Check if the search term is on the banned word list
  • Try simpler or more common words
  • Verify your internet connection

Coloring page isn't working?

  • Try a different image if one doesn't convert well
  • Clear the app cache and try again

Forgot your parent password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the parent login screen. You'll receive a secure reset link via email.

Inappropriate content appeared?

While rare, if you encounter inappropriate content, please report it immediately. This helps us improve filtering for all users.
