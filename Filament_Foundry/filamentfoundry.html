What It Does

App Overview

Filament Foundry helps you precisely track the weight of your 3D print filament spools before and after each print job, so you always know how much filament remains. This prevents starting large print jobs without sufficient material and lets you monitor usage by job.

Easily manage your entire filament spool library within the app and generate detailed usage reports designed to help 3D printing businesses optimize material purchasing and inventory control.

Currently the app relies on manually entered weights and automatically compensates for empty spool weight. An upcoming feature will enable real-time filament usage tracking with a rotary encoder, making management even more effortless.

What's Included

Features

⚖️

Weight Tracking

Log filament weight before and after each job to always know exactly how much remains on every spool.

🧮

Spool Tare Compensation

Automatically subtracts empty spool weight for precise net filament measurements every time.

🗂️

Spool Library

Manage your entire filament inventory — brands, colors, materials, and remaining quantities in one place.

📊

Usage Reports

Generate detailed reports for business insights, inventory planning, and material cost tracking.

🎯

Job-Level Tracking

See exactly how much filament each print job consumed — useful for quoting and cost analysis.

🔄

Rotary Encoder Support Coming Soon

Real-time filament length tracking via rotary encoder — no more manual weight entries.
