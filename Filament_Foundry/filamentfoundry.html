App Overview

Filament Foundry helps you precisely track the weight of your 3D print filament spools before and after each print job, so you always know how much filament remains. This prevents starting large print jobs without sufficient material and allows you to monitor filament usage by job. In addition, easily manage your entire filament spool library within the app. Generate detailed usage reports designed to help 3D printing businesses optimize material purchasing and inventory control. Currently, the app relies on manually entered weights and automatically compensates for empty spool weight. An upcoming feature will enable real-time filament usage tracking with a rotary encoder, making management even more effortless.

Features

  • Track filament weight usage to monitor remaining filament accurately.
  • Automatic compensation for empty spool weight to ensure precise measurements.
  • Manage and organize your filament spool library with ease.
  • Generate detailed usage reports for business insights and inventory management.
  • Future update will include rotary encoder support for real-time filament length tracking.
  • Intuitive interface designed for accuracy and ease of use.
