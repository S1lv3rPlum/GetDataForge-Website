What It Is

About The Juke

The Juke was created for Nashville North events to give fans a fun, digital way to browse available songs, send requests, and tip the band — without standing in line or shouting over the crowd.

The app uses no accounts, no passwords, and no personal profiles — just simple, fast song requests. Payments are handled via Venmo or cash, completely outside the app.

For Performers & Venues

Bring The Juke to Your Band or Venue 🎤

Want to let your crowd request songs, tip the band, and stay engaged all night? The Juke includes a companion Band & Venue Manager App coming soon to Google Play.

With the manager app, performers and venues will be able to:

  • 🎵 Build and customize your own live song list
  • 🕹️ Enable or disable requests in real time during shows
  • 📋 Update set lists instantly — no re-uploading or rebuilding
  • 📱 Manage multiple events, rotating bands, or weekly lineups
  • ⚡ Sync updates instantly with your audience's Juke app

No coding, no custom builds — just download, set up your profile, and you're ready to go.
