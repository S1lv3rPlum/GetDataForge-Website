Privacy Policy — Band & Venue Manager App

Last updated: January 2025

The Juke Band & Venue Manager App allows performers and venues to manage song lists, events, and audience requests. We take privacy seriously and are committed to keeping your information safe. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account / Profile Information: Band or venue name, email address, and optional profile details collected via Firebase Authentication.
  • Set Lists & Song Data: Songs you make available for requests, including metadata such as artist name, song title, and display order.
  • Event Data: Event dates, location names, and performance details you enter into the app.
  • Device Information: Basic device details such as model, OS version, and app version — used only to improve app performance.
  • Crash Logs & Analytics: Anonymous diagnostic data used to fix bugs and improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and allow access across devices.
  • To display your set lists and song availability to fans using the audience-facing Juke app.
  • To manage and update events or multiple venues under your account.
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance the user experience.

3. Public-Facing Data

Your set list and currently active songs are visible to fans using the audience-facing Juke app. This is the core function of the service. Your account email and personal profile details are never displayed publicly.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Your set list and active songs are visible to fans using the audience Juke app.
  • Anonymous app analytics may be sent to performance monitoring tools.

5. Data Retention

Set lists and event data are stored as long as your account is active. You may update or delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We protect your data using Firebase's built-in security rules and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The Band & Venue Manager App is intended for use by adult performers and venue operators and is not directed toward children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this page with a revised date.
